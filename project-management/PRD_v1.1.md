# 🌿 ISEYAA TiarionX — Product Requirements Document (PRD v1.1)

**Project Title:** ISEYAA (Geo-Intelligent State Operating System)  
**Region:** Ogun State, Nigeria (20 Local Government Areas)  
**Vision:** Transforming Ogun State into the world's most connected cultural and economic node via AI-driven human-centric technology.

---

## 1. STRATEGIC POSITIONING
ISEYAA is not a "super-app"; it is a **Distributed State Operating System**.  
Every Local Government Area (LGA) becomes a digital economic node where history, culture, and tourism assets are real-time, queryable data layers.

## 2. THE 20 LGA COVERAGE (DIGITIZATION SCOPE)
ISEYAA will provide full "Digital Life" coverage for all 20 LGAs:
- **Egba Region:** Abeokuta North, Abeokuta South, Ewekoro, Ifo, Obafemi Owode, Odeda.
- **Ijebu Region:** Ijebu Ode, Ijebu North, Ijebu North East, Ijebu East, Odogbolu, Ogun Waterside.
- **Remo Region:** Sagamu, Ikenne, Remo North.
- **Yewa Region:** Egbado North, Egbado South, Imeko Afon, Ipokia.
- **Industrial Corridor:** Ado-Odo/Ota.

---

## 3. CORE FUNCTIONAL MODULES

### 🛡️ Module 1: LGA Intelligence Service (The Brain)
- **Functions:** Store historical origin stories, traditional ruler maps, and economic data.
- **Case Study (Ijebu Igbo):** Headquarters of Ijebu North; known for the Orimolusi monarchy and forest-based agriculture. 
- **Tech:** Knowledge Graph linking Events → Locations → Hotels → Transport.

### 🎭 Module 2: Tourism & Culture Engine
- **Functions:** Attractions directory (Olumo Rock, Ojude Oba, etc.), Geo-tagging, AR storytelling.
- **AI Agent:** "Deep" Itinerary Planner v2 (generates multi-day travel plans).

### 🎫 Module 3: Events & Ticketing System
- **Functions:** Full lifecycle ticket creation, QR/NFC validation, pricing engine.
- **Squad:** Squad Delta (QA/Financial Compliance).

### 🏠 Module 4: Accommodation System (StayHub)
- **Functions:** Property onboarding, booking engine, reviews, and short-let management.
- **Integration:** Real-time availability linked to tourism traffic patterns.

### 🚕 Module 5: Mobility & Transport (MoveHub)
- **Functions:** Ride-hailing (Car, Bike, Boat), Route optimization for Ogun's specific road networks.
- **AI Agent:** Surge pricing and demand prediction.

### 🛒 Module 6: Enterprise Marketplace
- **Functions:** SME/Artisan onboarding, product listings, order management.
- **Economic Value:** Connecting village crafts to global buyers.

### 💳 Module 7: Wallet & Wealth Infrastructure
- **Functions:** Multi-currency wallet, Govt levy automation, Escrow for marketplace transactions.
- **Squad:** Squad Alpha (Core/Financial Security).

### 🏛️ Module 8: Government Command Dashboard
- **Functions:** Real-time revenue analytics per LGA, policy insights, cultural preservation archives.

---

## 4. MULTI-AGENT AI ARCHITECTURE
ISEYAA operates via a **Distributed Multi-Agent System (MAS)**:
1. **Orchestrator Agent:** The brain—coordinates tasks between squads/services.
2. **Data Intelligence Agent (Crawler):** Scrapes blogs, news, and social media to find "Ogun Signals".
3. **LGA Intelligence Agent:** Maintains the historical accuracy of the 20 regional profiles.
4. **Tourism Recommendation Agent:** Personalized "Deep" suggestions.
5. **Government Intelligence Agent:** Analyzes revenue and compliance.

---

## 5. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE (TEAM-READY)
- **Frontend:** Next.js (Squad Beta).
- **Backend:** Python FastAPI (Squad Alpha).
- **Database:** PostgreSQL + Supabase (Squad Alpha).
- **Communication:** Kafka / Pub-Sub for real-time agent signals.
- **DevOps:** CI/CD for auto-deployment to Render/Railway/Vercel (Squad Delta).

---

## 📅 SUCCESS METRICS (KPIs)
- **LGA Adoption Rate:** % of Ogun State LGAs fully digitized.
- **Economic Velocity:** Total transaction volume across marketplace/tourism.
- **Cultural Preservation Index:** Number of historical documents/oral traditions digitized.
- **AI Accuracy:** Relevance of "Deep" travel recommendations and crawler data.
